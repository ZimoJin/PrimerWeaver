Introduction to PrimerWeaver

Overview

PrimerWeaver is a comprehensive, integrated web server for primer design across multiple molecular biology workflows. Whether you're performing restriction enzyme cloning, Golden Gate assembly, Gibson assembly, multiplex PCR, or site-directed mutagenesis, PrimerWeaver provides a integrate platform that streamlines primer design and validation.

Built with modern molecular biology in mind, PrimerWeaver combines intelligent primer design algorithms with robust in-silico quality control (QC) checks. All calculations are performed in your browser (100% client-side), ensuring your data remains completely private and secure.

Key Features

Advanced QC Analysis

Comprehensive primer quality assessment including thermodynamic calculations, secondary structure prediction, self-dimer and cross-dimer detection.

Multiplex Pooling

Automatic primer pooling for multiplex PCR using graph-coloring algorithms, ensuring compatible primers within each pool.

Multiple Cloning Strategies

Support for Overlap PCR, Site-Directed Mutagenesis, Restriction/Ligation based cloning, USER Cloning, Golden Gate Assembly, and Gibson Assembly—all from a single interface.

100% Client-Side Computation

No data is uploaded or stored on servers. All primer design and QC analysis happens locally in your browser for maximum privacy.

Workflow Overview

The typical PrimerWeaver workflow follows these steps:

  1. Select a Module: Choose the cloning or amplification strategy that matches your experimental needs.
  2. Input Sequences: Provide your target sequence(s) in FASTA or plain text format. PrimerWeaver supports both single and multi-fragment assemblies.
  3. Configure Parameters: Adjust design parameters such as target melting temperature, GC content, primer length, and other strategy-specific settings.
  4. Design or Validate Primers: Either design new primers from scratch or validate existing primer sets using the integrated QC module.
  5. Review Results: Examine detailed metrics, secondary structures, dimer scores, and other QC indicators to ensure primer quality.
  6. Export and Order: Download your primer sequences and place orders with your preferred oligo synthesis vendor.

Available Modules

Primer QC

A utility to check the quality and compatibility of any primer sequences. Perfect for validating primers from publications, previous experiments, or colleagues before ordering. Calculates melting temperatures, GC content, secondary structures, and cross-dimer potential.

Overlap PCR (OE-PCR)

Design primers for Splicing by Overlap Extension PCR, a method to stitch DNA fragments together using complementary primer tails. Ideal for seamless joining of two or more fragments.

Site-Directed Mutagenesis

Design primers for introducing point mutations, insertions, or deletions into plasmids using overlap PCR. Supports various mutation formats including amino acid modifications and DNA-level changes.

Multiplex PCR

Design optimal primers for multiple targets in a single PCR reaction. Includes automatic pooling to minimize cross-dimer formation and ensure compatible annealing temperatures across all primers in each pool.

Restriction Enzyme Cloning

Traditional restriction enzyme-based cloning using specific Type II endonucleases. Select enzymes, design primers with appropriate recognition sites and spacer sequences.

USER Cloning

Uracil-Specific Excision Reagent (USER) cloning for ligation-independent assembly. Automatically incorporates uracil residues in primers to create long, specific overhangs after USER digestion.

Golden Gate Assembly

Type IIS enzyme-based assembly for seamless, ordered multi-part cloning. Supports BsaI, BsmBI, BbsI, and other Type IIS enzymes.

Gibson Assembly

Overlap-directed assembly for flexible multi-fragment cloning. Design primers with optimized 5' homology arms (typically 15–30 bp) flanking each fragment, with uniform melting temperatures for efficient assembly.

Why PrimerWeaver?

Integrated Platform: No need to switch between multiple tools for different cloning methods. All strategies are integrated into one interface with consistent design principles.
Thermodynamically Accurate: Uses unified nearest-neighbor thermodynamics with explicit support for monovalent (Na⁺) and divalent (Mg²⁺) cations, following SantaLucia et al. and modern thermodynamic databases.
Production-Ready: Built-in quality checks (secondary structure, dimer detection, fidelity scoring) ensure your primers will work reliably in the lab, reducing failed experiments.
Privacy-First Design: All computations are 100% client-side. Your sequences never leave your computer. No logins, no cookies, no data storage on servers.
Educational Resource: Detailed documentation and step-by-step explanations help you understand the principles of primer design, thermodynamics, and assembly strategies.

Getting Started

To begin designing primers:

  1. Click "Launch App" in the top navigation bar.
  2. Select your desired module from the dashboard.
  3. Upload or paste your sequence(s) and configure your parameters.
  4. Click "Design" or "Run QC" to analyze.
  5. Review the results and export your primers as needed.

For more detailed information on any module, visit the Documents section in the main navigation menu.
