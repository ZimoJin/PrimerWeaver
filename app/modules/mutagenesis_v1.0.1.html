Target DNA sequence ?

Paste or upload a DNA sequence (FASTA or plain text). Coding or non-coding regions are supported; ORFs and reading frames are detected automatically. Use Reverse complement if the input is from the reverse strand. Whitespace and line breaks are ignored.
Mutation mode:

Amino-acid edits ?

Positions are 1-based in the translated ORF.

Examples:

  • V55A: Start=55, New=A (single AA swap)
  • V55ASA: Start=55, New=ASA (1 AA → 3 AAs)
  • VKL[55-57]→GA: Start=55, End=57, New=GA (3 AAs → 2 AAs)
  • Insert after 55: Operation=Insertion, Start=55, New=GGG
  • Delete 55-60: Operation=Deletion, Start=55, End=60

DNA sequence edits ?

Positions are 1-based in the input DNA sequence.

Use Deletion, Replacement, or Insertion. The “Current sequence” column previews what is at the selected range in the current template.

Examples:

  • Deletion: Start=100, End=107 (removes 8 bp)
  • Replacement: Start=100, End=107, New=ATGC (replaces 8 bp with 4 bp)
  • Insertion: Start=100, New=ATGC (inserts after position 100; End can be blank)

Display note: For insertions, the original sequence view shows highlighted -- placeholders to indicate the inserted segment position.
